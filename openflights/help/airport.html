Context Help: Airports

"From" is the airport your flight took off from.

"To" is the airport your flight landed at.


If you have already flown to the airport, you can click on its icon in the map, or choose it from the drop-down box.


If you need to enter a new airport, there are two ways to find it:

  1. Enter the city name (eg. "Singapore") or the airport code (3-letter IATA, "SIN", or 4-letter ICAO, "WSSS") into the search box, and press TAB or ENTER. A list of matching airports will appear in the drop down box.
  2. Click on or to pop up a search dialog, where you can find the airport by airport name, city name, country name or airport code.
To enter a return flight (the current airports in reverse order), click on the Swap button .
